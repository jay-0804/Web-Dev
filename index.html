Delicious Pancakes

Fluffy and easy-to-make pancakes for a perfect breakfast

Ingredients Instructions Tips

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of flour
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup of milk
  • 1/4 cup of sugar
  • 1 teaspoon of baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/4 cup of melted butter

Instructions

  1. Mix the flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt in a large bowl.
  2. In another bowl, whisk the eggs, then add milk and melted butter.
  3. Pour wet ingredients into dry ingredients and stir until combined (some lumps are okay).
  4. Heat a non-stick skillet over medium heat and lightly grease it.
  5. Pour 1/4 cup of batter per pancake. Cook until bubbles form and edges look set.
  6. Flip pancakes and cook for another 1-2 minutes until golden brown.
  7. Serve warm with maple syrup, fresh fruit, or whipped cream.

Tips

  • For fluffier pancakes, fold in beaten egg whites at the end.
  • Use buttermilk instead of regular milk for richer flavor.
  • Don't press pancakes with the spatula; let them rise naturally.
  • Keep skillet at medium heat to cook evenly without burning.
